Who we are

Built in Lagos.
Trusted globally.

Swappit Consulting LTD is a technology consulting and product engineering firm headquartered in Lagos, Nigeria — serving clients across Africa, Europe, North America, and beyond. We combine deep technical execution with business-savvy consulting to help organisations navigate complex digital challenges.

From early-stage startups to established enterprises, we bring strategic thinking, clean code, and genuine care to every engagement — no matter where in the world you are. We do not just build software — we build outcomes.
